As a part of our onboarding process, all students perform an evaluation to establish where they stand according to their grade. This will also serve as the basis to create an individualized learning plan for each student.

Individualized
learning plan

We prepare an individualized learning plan for each student, establishing measurable goals. Tutoring is not only about solving short-term needs but about providing each student with the necessary tools to understand mathematical concepts to foster long-term learning. Through an individualized learning plan, we make sure both the short and long-term needs are well served.
